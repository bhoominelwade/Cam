astlin rejected a colorful/boxy first preview as "bad" and set the direction: **look like the iPhone camera app + liquid glass — very modern, sleek, minimal.**

**Why:** Cam should feel like a native extension of the iOS camera, not a third-party gadget. Restraint reads as quality in this category.

**How to apply:** dark viewfinder-first UI; hairline thirds grid (white ~16%, sub-1px); face indicator = soft rounded corner brackets (white; iOS green #30D158 on celebrate), never a hard rectangle; glass surfaces everywhere chrome is needed (blur + translucent fill rgba(80,80,85,0.32) + hairline white border + top specular); one thin iOS-yellow (#FFD60A) chevron cue; capsule/pill shapes; SF-style typography, tabular numerals; camera chrome mimics iOS (mode labels in caps w/ yellow active, shutter ring). Avoid: saturated fills, thick strokes, busy labels, more than one accent at a time. Applies to S6 polish and every UI addition; the claude.ai preview artifact and `app/src/overlay/*` embody it.
